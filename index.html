What Sift does on the homepage

Sift is not a novelty chatbot. It acts like a front-door intelligence layer that detects intent and guides the visitor into the strongest next step.

Identifies the visitor

Homepage Sift starts with the right split: are you here to find a role or hire talent? That makes the experience feel guided immediately.

Uses guided replies

Instead of making people type from scratch, Sift offers relevant quick actions like browse roles, create profile, start hiring, or understand TalentBuzz.

Routes to action

Sift recommends the best route based on intent: go to roles, profile setup, recruiter contact, or employer intake so momentum is never lost.

Two high-value paths. One intelligent guide.

The homepage should feel premium for both sides of the market. Sift supports each path differently while keeping the experience focused and structured.
