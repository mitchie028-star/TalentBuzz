The Problem

Too much hiring still depends on resumes that do not prove real fit.

Companies lose time reviewing weak-fit profiles, while strong candidates can easily be overlooked. TalentBuzz is designed to improve signal before the process gets expensive.

Every low-quality interview slows momentum and increases the cost of hiring.
