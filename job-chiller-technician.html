Chiller Technician

📍 Manufacturing / Field Support | 🏭 Onsite

Role Overview

Perform preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair work for HVAC and chiller systems while ensuring compliance with safety and industry standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct routine servicing and preventive maintenance of HVAC and chiller systems.
  • Diagnose and troubleshoot air conditioning and refrigeration issues.
  • Repair or replace HVAC and chiller components as needed.
  • Maintain accurate service and repair records.
  • Provide responsive technical support and contribute to process improvements.

Qualifications

  • At least 3 years of relevant experience as an HVAC or Chiller Technician.
  • Strong technical knowledge in installation, maintenance, and repair of HVAC systems.
  • Can read and interpret technical manuals and schematics.
  • Strong troubleshooting and analytical skills.
  • Valid driver’s license and reliable transportation is preferred.
