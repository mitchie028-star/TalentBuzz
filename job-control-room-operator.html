Control Room Operator

📍 Sta. Maria, Bulacan | 🏭 Onsite | 💰 PHP 20,000–21,000 + OT

Role Overview

Support boiler operations by monitoring parts and auxiliary systems, helping ensure safe operation and timely preventive maintenance.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor, check, and inspect boiler parts and auxiliary equipment.
  • Help ensure boilers remain in good operating condition.
  • Support repair work based on preventive maintenance schedules.
  • Identify parts requiring repair and coordinate during breakdowns.
  • Assist in ISO compliance and audit-related activities.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering.
  • At least 1 year of experience as an operator in manufacturing.
  • Knowledge in machine operation and plant support work.
  • Strong sense of responsibility and equipment awareness.
