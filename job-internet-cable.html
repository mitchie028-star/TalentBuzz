Customer Experience Representative – Internet & Cable

📍 ResultsCX – Alaska Campus, Pasig | 🏢 Onsite | 🎓 At least SHS Graduate | 💰 PHP 17,200–19,200 Package + Complexity Pay

Role Overview

Join an Internet & Cable account as a Customer Experience Representative and help customers with inquiries, service concerns, and account-related concerns across support channels. This role is ideal for candidates with strong communication skills, good computer navigation, and a customer-first mindset.

Compensation & Benefits

  • PHP 17,200 package for candidates with 6 to 23 months of experience
  • PHP 19,200 package for candidates with 24 months and up of experience
  • Complexity pay:
    • PHP 3,000 for 6 to 23 months experience
    • PHP 6,000 for 24 months and up experience
  • Average monthly incentive: PHP 10,000 to PHP 40,000

Key Qualifications

  • At least a Senior High School graduate
  • At least 6 months of contact center experience
  • Strong communication skills
  • Basic computer navigation skills
  • Ability to type at least 35 words per minute is a plus

What We’re Looking For

  • Customer-focused and solution-oriented mindset
  • Ability to multitask across multiple screens and systems
  • Positive attitude and strong work ethic
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced onsite support environment
