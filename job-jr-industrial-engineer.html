Junior Industrial Engineer

📍 Manufacturing Site | 🏭 Onsite

Role Overview

Support production reporting, standards development, and process improvement initiatives through data gathering, validation, and analysis.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collect, verify, and check data related to individual production reports.
  • Support work measurement and methods improvement activities.
  • Help establish production standards such as cycle time, capacity, and manpower ratios.
  • Assist in preparing production and plant analysis reports.
  • Support documentation and quality records management for the IE department.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Industrial Engineering.
  • At least 1 to 3 years of relevant work experience.
  • Background in documentation and data encoding within manufacturing is an advantage.
  • Computer literate and familiar with MS Office applications.
