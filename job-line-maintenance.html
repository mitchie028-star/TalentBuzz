Line Maintenance

📍 Bulacan / Valenzuela | 🏭 Onsite | 💰 Approx. PHP 18,070 Monthly

Role Overview

Provide technical maintenance support by inspecting, repairing, and troubleshooting production machines to help minimize downtime and keep operations running smoothly.

Key Responsibilities

  • Inspect and repair machines based on job orders from operators.
  • Perform rewiring of control panels and assist in machine operation after repair.
  • Troubleshoot machine breakdowns and restore equipment functionality.
  • Maintain good housekeeping in assigned work areas.
  • Perform related maintenance tasks as assigned.

Qualifications

  • Technical, vocational, or trade school graduate; bachelor’s degree is also accepted.
  • At least 1 to 3 years of experience in a manufacturing environment.
  • Background in electronic or electromechanical work is preferred.
  • Strong technical and analytical troubleshooting skills.
