Medical Billing Customer Support Representative

🏥 Healthcare Account | 💰 PHP 25,000–33,000 | 📞 Voice / 💬 Non-Voice Support

Role Overview

Join a healthcare support team as a Medical Billing Customer Support Representative, assisting patients with billing inquiries, insurance concerns, and payment-related questions. This role is ideal for candidates with experience in healthcare revenue cycle management (RCM) who can deliver clear, empathetic, and compliant support.

Key Responsibilities

  • Handle inbound and outbound calls, emails, and chats related to billing and insurance inquiries
  • Assist patients in understanding statements, charges, and coverage details
  • Research and resolve billing discrepancies, denials, and claim rejections
  • Document all interactions accurately while ensuring compliance (HIPAA, TCPA)
  • Escalate complex issues to senior billing or compliance teams when needed
  • Educate patients on payment plans, benefits, and financial assistance options
  • Meet performance targets such as resolution time, accuracy, and customer satisfaction

Key Qualifications

  • 1–2 years of experience in medical billing, healthcare RCM, or customer support
  • Strong problem-solving skills with ability to explain complex billing concepts clearly
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to handle sensitive patient information with professionalism and confidentiality
  • Knowledge of healthcare billing processes and insurance workflows is an advantage

What We’re Looking For

  • Empathetic and patient-focused communication style
  • High attention to detail and accuracy
  • Ability to work in a compliance-driven environment
  • Dependable and able to handle sensitive cases professionally
