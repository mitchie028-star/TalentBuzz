Mould Foreman

📍 Sta. Maria, Bulacan | 🏭 Onsite | 💰 Basic PHP 25,000–27,000 | Total Package up to PHP 43,875

Role Overview

Lead mould maintenance operations and ensure mould reliability through preventive and corrective work, technical oversight, and team coordination.

Key Responsibilities

  • Oversee preventive and corrective maintenance of mould equipment.
  • Ensure adherence to procedures, work instructions, and standard process.
  • Provide technical support to the mould maintenance team.
  • Monitor spare parts inventory and support audit and ISO compliance activities.
  • Manage manpower schedule and task assignment.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.
  • At least 2 to 3 years of maintenance operations experience.
  • Knowledge in preventive and corrective maintenance of machines.
  • Strong communication, reporting, and leadership skills.
