Mould Technician

📍 Manufacturing Site | 🏭 Onsite

Role Overview

Maintain moulds and related accessories in good working condition through repair, cleaning, safekeeping, and preventive maintenance support.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and safeguard moulds and mould accessories on designated racks.
  • Perform mould cleaning and repair work as needed.
  • Conduct inventory of moulds and accessories.
  • Support preventive maintenance scheduling for mould equipment.
  • Carry out related technical tasks as assigned.

Qualifications

  • High school, vocational, or technical school graduate.
  • At least 6 months to 1 year of relevant work experience.
  • Knowledge of mechanical inventory and mould handling is an advantage.
  • Detail-oriented with strong equipment care discipline.
