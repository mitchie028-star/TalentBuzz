Pet Care Advisor

📍 Makati | 🏢 Onsite | 🐶 Pet-Friendly Workplace | 🌱 Wellness Retail

Role Overview

Turn your passion for pets into a meaningful career. As a Pet Care Advisor, you will guide pet parents in making informed decisions about their pets’ health, nutrition, and overall well-being. This role blends customer experience, education, and retail operations in a purpose-driven environment.

What You’ll Do

  • Educate pet parents on nutrition, wellness, and product selection
  • Create personalized and memorable in-store customer experiences
  • Support daily store operations and maintain product presentation
  • Drive sales while building long-term customer relationships
  • Contribute to team initiatives and continuous improvement programs

Why Join

  • Be part of a leading all-natural lifestyle and pet care brand
  • Work in a pet-friendly environment with a strong, positive culture
  • Access continuous learning, mentorship, and development programs
  • Grow into leadership roles through structured career pathways
  • Join a company known for high retention and employee satisfaction

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in any field (Animal Science, Veterinary Medicine, or related courses is a plus)
  • Must be a pet owner and genuinely passionate about animals
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Customer-focused with confidence and initiative
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced retail environment
  • Non-smoker preferred (aligned with wellness culture)

Compensation & Benefits

  • Sales incentives and performance bonuses
  • Paid vacation and sick leaves
  • Life and medical insurance
  • Employee discounts and monthly product allowance
  • Attendance incentives
  • Training, mentorship, and career growth support

Who This Role Is Perfect For

  • Pet lovers who want to turn passion into a career
  • Fresh graduates looking for a purpose-driven role
  • Retail professionals interested in wellness and lifestyle brands
  • Individuals who enjoy helping others and building relationships
