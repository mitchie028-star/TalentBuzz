Production Foreman

📍 Manufacturing Site | 🏭 Onsite

Role Overview

Oversee production operations, ensuring efficiency, quality, and manpower coordination.

Key Responsibilities

  • Supervise production activities and staff
  • Ensure materials availability and workflow
  • Coordinate with QA and Maintenance teams
  • Monitor production targets and output
  • Evaluate team performance

Qualifications

  • Engineering or related degree
  • 3–5 years manufacturing experience
  • Supervisory experience required
  • Strong leadership and coordination skills
