Raw Materials Custodian

📍 Sta. Maria, Bulacan | 🏭 Onsite | 💰 Approx. PHP 18,070 Monthly

Role Overview

Manage raw material inventory, documentation, and stock monitoring to ensure timely material availability for plant operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor daily stock levels and ensure availability for production use.
  • Maintain proper storage, location, and condition of raw materials and supplies.
  • Prepare daily, weekly, and monthly reports for inventory and variance monitoring.
  • Support requisition planning for materials nearing critical stock levels.
  • Maintain records and support operational improvement initiatives in the section.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in any business-related course.
  • At least 3 to 5 years of experience in warehouse or inventory work within manufacturing.
  • Knowledgeable in warehouse systems and documentation.
  • Computer literate with strong analytical and reporting skills.
