Role Overview

Ensure workplace safety compliance, conduct training, and minimize operational risks.

  • Conduct safety inspections and audits

  • Investigate incidents and recommend actions
  • Provide safety training and awareness

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • 2–3 years safety experience
  • Knowledge of safety standards
