Truck Mechanic

📍 Operations / Fleet | 🏭 Onsite

Role Overview

Diagnose, repair, and maintain trucks, forklifts, and heavy equipment to support safe and efficient fleet operations with minimal downtime.

Key Responsibilities

  • Diagnose and repair mechanical, electrical, and hydraulic issues in trucks and heavy equipment.
  • Perform scheduled maintenance and inspections.
  • Coordinate repairs with operations to minimize equipment downtime.
  • Maintain detailed service records and parts usage documentation.
  • Follow safety procedures and stay current on relevant tools and technologies.

Qualifications

  • At least 3 years of experience as a truck or heavy equipment mechanic.
  • Strong diagnostic and problem-solving skills.
  • Knowledge of hydraulic systems, electrical systems, and engine mechanics.
  • Can read and interpret technical manuals and schematics.
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills.
