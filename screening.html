How This Works

Questions come from the role's saved screening_config.
Critical and high-weight questions influence the score more heavily.
Knockout questions can auto-screen out an application when the answer does not meet the role requirement.
Once submitted, your application is created together with the screening result and score.

Answering Tips

Be concrete: mention tools, years, scope, environment, and business outcome.
For proof or simulation questions, explain what you personally did.
Short or generic answers may lower your score even if they are technically correct.
